1. ✅ Chatbot Functions
Answer common questions
Provide basic info (hours, location, contact)
Guide users through simple steps
Escalate to human agent when needed

2. 🗣️ Common Questions & Responses
User Says	Bot Responds
"Hi" / "Hello"	"Hi! How can I help you today?"
"What are your hours?"	"We're open Mon–Fri, 9 AM–6 PM."
"Where are you located?"	"Our office is at 2722 Michelson Dr #100, Irvine, CA 92612"
"Help with service"	"Can you tell me more about the issue?"
